About Me

My name is Christian Lantigua and I am 20 years old with a Bachelors degree in Digital Arts and Sciences. I truly believe that what we do in the world of Digital Art is magical. I can create scenes from my imagination, I can convey emotion through my art, and I can help others achieve their dreams as well. I enjoy helping people, and making their imagination come to life is the most wonderful gift of all. That, coupled with a nice sense of humor is what makes me a great addition to any working team. I'm also easy to talk to and make friends easily. My previous work in the Digital Arts program shows that I have a good understanding of design techniques that would make for great logos, advertisements, or even animation. Continue on to see some of the work I have produced in the Digital Arts program, and if you have any questions or think I might make a good addition to your team, please feel free to Contact Me.
